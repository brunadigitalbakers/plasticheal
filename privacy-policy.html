Our principles

The UAB Research Park applies the principles for protection of data established by Article 5 of the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation EU 2016/679 dated 27 April 2016), and specifically:

  • We will only process your personal data in a valid lawful manner, provided you give your consent which we will always require in a clear and transparent way or in those cases where it is required for the execution of the contracts you participate in.
  • We will only process the data that is necessary to comply with our purposes and we will not make any other use.
  • In order to guarantee that the data is updated, you may manage the updating of your personal data by contacting our Privacy Office.
  • The data will only be stored for the time required to comply with the purposes for which it was collected.
  • We apply security measures, including data encryption, to guarantee data integrity and confidentiality, and the protection of your personal data against loss, destruction or other damage.

Purposes for the processing of your personal data

The UAB Research Park collects and processes personal data for the following purposes:

  • Provided we have the permission of the interested party, issue information about the PLASTICHEAL project, activities and services, especially, through a "Newsletter" and our informative communications.

Legal bases that allow us to process personal data

  • The express consent of the interested parties.
  • Whenever processing is required to comply with an applicable legal obligation.

What personal data do we process?

We only collect minimal contact data such as your name, e-mail address, telephone, and the entity to which you belong in order to send our "newsletters" and communications and keep you informed about the PLASTICHEAL project.

We may also collect your personal data whenever you request our services or collaborate with us through forms or e-mail messages, always provided you so state. and also whenever you sign a contract with us. We will always ensure we process the minimum data required and store it for the time strictly necessary to manage our collaboration or provide services and remain in contact for future collaboration projects.

Who do we share your personal data with?

We will share your personal data with the PLASTICHEAL project partners, as coresponsible of the data treatment:

  • Finnish Institute of Occupational Health
  • Wageningen University
  • Technical University of Denmark
  • French Alternative Energies and Atomic Energy Commission
  • National Institute of Health and Medical Research
  • Foundation for Health Training and Research of the Region of Murcia
  • Plastics Technology Centre
  • University of Manchester
  • Helmholtz Centre for Environmental Research
  • Leipzig University

Some of our partners may be located in third countries outside the European Union (EU) or the European Economic Area (EEA). In all these cases there is contractual assurance that guarantees adequate security measures, as well as compliance with this Policy and the General Data Protection Regulation.

Data on minors

Minors under 16 years of age may only use our portals with permission from their parents or legal representatives (tutors), who then assume responsibility.

Your rights and how to exercise them

You may exercise your rights relative to your personal data and privacy at any time and free of charge, as established in applicable regulations, including:

  • Knowing which personal data (yours) is being processed by the UAB Research Park, for what purpose, and with which third parties it is shared;
  • Obtain a copy this data (the second and successive copies may generate administrative costs);
  • Demand rectification of inaccurate personal data or that incomplete data be updated;
  • Demand the suppression of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purpose(s) for which it was collected;
  • Demand limitation on the treatment of your personal data whenever its potential inaccuracy is verified and in other cases described in the applicable regulations;
  • The right that your personal data be transmitted to another company in a common structured format (right to the portability of your data); or
  • The right to oppose the treatment of your personal data by UAB Research Park.

You may exercise these rights by contacting our Privacy Office in writing at the following e-mail address: dpo.parc@uab.cat
